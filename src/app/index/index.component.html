About Us

International School is the center for excellence. The school runs under the Indian Technical Computer Institute (ITCI) trust which is registered under trust and society act, ITCI formed in the year 1996 with educational bylaws.
ITCI started the international school in the year 2009-2010 with the vision of quality education. ITCI’ CIS is registered under Government of Maharashtra SSC Board and proposed Central Government, CBSE Board. We have taken an initiative towards the progress of India by offering best global practices in children through an integrated research based international curriculum. Our aim is to enhance and develop the capabilities of the students to the maximum. We believe in the holistic development of the child and hence follow methodologies ensuring strong moral, social, emotional, and physical development.
